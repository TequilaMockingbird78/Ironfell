## Overview
A semi-arid steppe valley defined by rivers running lengthwise between hills and low mountains. Movement through the region follows water and stone rather than roads, creating natural corridors of travel and control.

## Geography
- Biome: Semi-arid steppe
- Terrain: Valley floor with bordering hills and low mountains
- Water: Multiple rivers running north–south
- Size: ~185 square miles (approx. 19.3 × 9.6 miles)

## Population
- Total population: ~4,472 humanoids
- Dominant race: Dwarves
- Density: Low (10–50 per square mile)

## Settlements
- Major strongholds: 1
- Minor strongholds: 1
- Villages: 8
- Hamlets / holds: 17

Settlements cluster along rivers, stone resources, and defensible hill-faces.

## Governance
- Structure: Feudal monarchy
- Ruler: One High Thane
- Local lords: Six vassal lords holding land and chartered rights
- Power alignment: True Neutral

Authority emphasizes continuity, obligation, and balance rather than ideology.

## Magic Norms
Magic is widespread and institutional. Mage guilds operate openly and are embedded in infrastructure, craft, and governance support.

## Religion
Religion is a regular but non-dominant part of life. Most settlements maintain a shrine or temple, often independent but loosely coordinated.
