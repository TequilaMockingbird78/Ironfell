# Region 002 — Northern Forest Plains (UNNAMED)

id: region_002  
type: region  
canon: hard

## Overview
A temperate forest region of deciduous and mixed woodland, interspersed with open grasslands and gently flattened plains. The land feels open and traversable, with fewer natural barriers than its southern neighbor.

## Geography
- Biome: Temperate forest (deciduous and mixed)
- Terrain: Plains with forest breaks and grassy expanses
- Size: Approximately 5.3 × 8.0 miles

## Inhabitants
- Dominant race: Humans
- Population density: Low (10–50 per square mile)
- Settlement pattern: Rural homesteads and small clustered communities

## Governance
- Government: Tribal confederation
- Power alignment: Lawful Neutral
Authority is collective, rule-bound, and negotiated among tribal leaders rather than imposed from a single seat.

## Magic Norms
Magic is widespread and institutionalized. Mage guilds and magical practitioners are integrated into daily life, particularly for agriculture, weather-watching, and mediation.

## Religion
Religion exists but is not dominant. Shrines and small temples are scattered, often maintained by families or communities rather than priestly hierarchies.

## Relations
- Borders:
  - South: region_001

## Notes
This region has no widely recognized central identity beyond tribal affiliation.
