# The Wayfarers’ Compact

id: faction_wayfarers_compact  
type: faction  
canon: hard

## Scope
Operates across multiple regions, including regions 001–004.

## Role
A loose association of traders, guides, messengers, and caravan leaders.

## Goals
- Keep routes open
- Share intelligence
- Avoid entanglement in local politics

## Fears
- Closed borders
- Militarized trade routes

## Red Lines
- Arbitrary seizure of goods
- Violence against caravans

## Notes
Information travels faster than goods within the Compact.
