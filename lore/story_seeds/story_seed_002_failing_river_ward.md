## Overview
A long-standing magical ward regulating a stretch of river flow has begun to behave unpredictably, causing irregular currents and flooding.

## Known Impact
- Unsafe river crossings
- Damage to nearby farmland
- Delays in trade and travel

## Involved Parties
- Local villagers dependent on the river
- Mage guild members responsible for upkeep
- River wardens attempting temporary measures

## Uncertainties
It is unclear whether the ward is degrading naturally, was improperly maintained, or has been interfered with.

## Current Status
The ward still functions intermittently.

## Resolution Conditions
Stability may be restored through repair, replacement, reworking, or deliberate removal.
