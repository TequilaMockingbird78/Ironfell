# River Wardens

id: faction_river_wardens  
type: faction  
canon: hard

## Role
Custodians of river crossings, flow rights, and flood control.

## Goals
- Keep rivers navigable and predictable
- Enforce crossing customs and tolls

## Fears
- Unregulated damming or diversion
- Political interference

## Red Lines
- Destruction of river infrastructure

## Notes
Neutral by charter, opinionated in practice.
