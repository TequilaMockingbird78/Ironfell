# Charter Holders

id: faction_charter_holders  
type: faction  
canon: hard

## Role
Holders of legal charters granting rights to mines, trade routes, or resources.

## Goals
- Protect charter privileges
- Expand holdings through precedent

## Fears
- Revocation of charters
- Rival claims

## Red Lines
- Charter nullification without legal process

## Notes
Legal power often rivals noble authority.
