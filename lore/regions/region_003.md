id: region_003

## Overview
A temperate wetland of marshes and bogs broken by rolling hills and gentle slopes. The land is damp, irregular, and deceptively fertile, supporting both isolation and growth.

## Geography
- Biome: Temperate wetlands (marshes and bogs)
- Terrain: Hills with low-lying wet areas
- Size: Approximately 28.5 × 28.5 miles

## Inhabitants
- Dominant race: Gnomes
- Population density: Moderate (50–100 per square mile)
- Settlement pattern: Hill communities, growing villages, and small market towns

## Governance
- Government: Feudal monarchy
- Power alignment: Chaotic Neutral
Authority exists but is fluid, shaped as much by personality and circumstance as by law.

## Magic Norms
Magic is widespread and thrives here. Magical institutions and informal traditions coexist, often blending experimentation with practicality.

## Religion
Religion is present but not dominant. Shrines and temples exist, but spiritual life is personal and varied rather than centralized.

## Notes
The region is developing and changing; its future shape is not yet settled.
