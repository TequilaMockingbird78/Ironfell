## Overview
A solitary bugbear has been raiding travelers and isolated hamlets near the hills bordering the valley corridor. The threat is sporadic but persistent.

## Known Impact
- Theft of food, tools, and trade goods
- Injuries to lone travelers
- Certain hill paths avoided after dusk

## Local Accounts
Descriptions vary:
- Some say the creature is cunning and selective
- Others believe it avoids unnecessary killing
- A few suggest it was driven into the valley by events elsewhere

## Area of Activity
- Hill paths and outcrops
- Abandoned stoneworks
- River crossings distant from villages

## Current Status
No coordinated response has yet been organized.

## Resolution Conditions
This situation may change if the creature is confronted, displaced, negotiated with, or eliminated.
