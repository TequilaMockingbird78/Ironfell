# Region 004 — Desert City-State (UNNAMED)

id: region_004  
type: region  
canon: hard

## Overview
A dry desert region with sparse vegetation, defined by gently sloping hills and arid conditions. Despite the harsh environment, organized settlement has taken root around a central city.

## Geography
- Biome: Dry desert
- Terrain: Hills and gentle slopes
- Size: Approximately 19.2 × 19.2 miles

## Inhabitants
- Dominant race: Halflings
- Population density: Medium, concentrated around a central city
- Settlement pattern: One major city-state with surrounding towns, villages, and farmsteads

## Governance
- Government: City-state republic
- Power alignment: Lawful Good
Governed by elected officials, councils, and public assemblies, emphasizing civic duty and order.

## Magic Norms
Magic is uncommon. Smaller settlements often lack spellcasters entirely. The central city hosts a limited number of practitioners.

## Religion
Religion is widespread and deeply influential. Shrines are common, and the city contains multiple temples that play a significant civic role.

## Relations
- Borders:
  - East: region_001

## Notes
The contrast between civic order and environmental hardship defines this region’s character.
