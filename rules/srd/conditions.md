# Conditions (Common)

id: rules_conditions_35e
type: rules
system: dnd35e

## Common Conditions

### Prone
- −4 penalty on melee attack rolls
- +4 bonus to AC against ranged attacks
- −4 penalty to AC against melee attacks
Standing up provokes an attack of opportunity.

### Grappled
- Limited movement
- −4 penalty to Dexterity
- Cannot make attacks of opportunity
Certain actions are restricted.

### Fatigued
- −2 to Strength and Dexterity
- Cannot run or charge
Becomes exhausted if further fatigued.

### Exhausted
- −6 to Strength and Dexterity
- Movement speed reduced
Rest is required to recover.

### Stunned
- Drops held items
- Loses Dexterity bonus to AC
- Cannot take actions

## Notes
Conditions are cumulative only if explicitly stated.
